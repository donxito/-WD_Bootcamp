## Sum of positive (8kyu)
https://www.codewars.com/kata/5715eaedb436cf5606000381

> You get an array of numbers, return the sum of all of the positives ones.
> Example [1,-4,7,12] => 1 + 7 + 12 = 20


Can solve with:
- loop (eg. forEach)
- arr.reduce()


## Square Every Digit (7kyu)
